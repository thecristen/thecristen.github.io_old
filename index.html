About this website

Well, the little picture at the upper left and its associated favicon are mine. As are the art works linked. No takey.

This website template is a modified version of Lagom. I built this website using Jekyll, the Ruby-based static site generator (according to this page, even Obama used it! Though maybe that's not such a good compliment anymore). I liked the idea of hosting all my own content (which I do on Webfaction), and I got sick of scouring the Internet for clean, simple Wordpress themes. Building it this way also allowed me to gain some new skills!

If you find something broken and it really bothers you, feel free to open up an issue on Github.
